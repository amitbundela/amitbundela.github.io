Welcome

Hello and welcome to my website.

I am Amit Bundela, a Technical Officer in the Department of Energy Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi). I have been associated with IIT Delhi since 2004, when I joined as a Junior Technical Officer in the Department of Civil & Enviromental Engineering at institute. Over the years, I have had the opportunity to contribute to a wide range of technical and information technology initiatives supporting the institute's academic, research, and administrative activities.

In recognition of my professional growth and contributions, I was promoted to the position of Deputy Technical Officer in 2016. In 2024, I was selected as a Technical Officer (Group A) through direct recruitment at IIT Delhi. My professional journey at the institute has provided me with valuable experience in information technology, web development, system administration, database management, and the deployment of technology-driven solutions for higher education and research environments.

I hold a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degree in Computer Science and Engineering, which I obtained in 2002 from Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, now known as Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur.

My areas of interest include web technologies, Linux and open-source software, networking, server administration, database systems, cloud technologies, and automation of institutional processes. I enjoy exploring new technologies and developing practical solutions that improve efficiency and user experience.

This website serves as a platform to share information about my professional activities, technical projects, interests, and experiences. Here, you will find details about my work, ongoing initiatives, and contributions in the field of information technology at IIT Delhi.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find the information useful and informative.
