Amit Bundela joined the institute in 2004 as a Junior Technical Officer. In 2022, he was promoted to Deputy Technical Officer. In 2024 he was selected as a Technical Officer (Group A) through direct recruitment at the institute. He works in the Department of Energy Science and Engineering at IIT Delhi.

He has received his Bachelor of Engineering (Computer Science & Engineering) in year 2002 from the then Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, now Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, Gorakhpur (U.P.) India.
